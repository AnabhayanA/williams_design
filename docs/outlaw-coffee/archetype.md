## Chosen Archetype: The Outlaw

### Definition and Core Traits
The Outlaw represents rebellion, nonconformity, and freedom. Appeals to those who want to stand apart and break the rules.

**Core Motivation:** Freedom, independence, authenticity.

**Key Personality Traits:**
- Bold and daring
- Defiant and disruptive
- Authentic and unapologetic

**Target Audience:**
Independent thinkers, night owls, creators, anti-corporate coffee drinkers.

### Visual Identity Guidelines
**Primary Colors:** Black, Deep Crimson  
**Secondary Colors:** Ash Gray, Burnt Copper  

**Typography:**  
- Headlines: Bold Sans-Serif (Bebas Neue, Impact)  
- Subtext: Rough Display/Handwritten (Rock Salt, Permanent Marker)  

**Imagery:**  
Urban or industrial environments, raw coffee photography, rebellious lifestyle visuals.
